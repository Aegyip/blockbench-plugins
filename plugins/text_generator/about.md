**Text Generator** by [Speaway](https://speaway.com) turns any text into Minecraft-style 3D cube geometry.

## Features
- Default Minecraft-style + curated block fonts + custom upload
- Size, depth (0 = paper-thin), spacing, alignment
- Colored outline border
- Bold, italic, underline, strikethrough
- Saveable presets
- Unicode and emoji support
- Double-click a Text group in the outliner to edit and regenerate

## How to use
Go to **Tools -> Text Generator**, enter your text, adjust settings, and click Generate.
